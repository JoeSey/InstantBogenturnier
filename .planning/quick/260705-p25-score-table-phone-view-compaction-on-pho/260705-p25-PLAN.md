<objective>
Compact ScoreTable.svelte for phone-portrait widths (below the app's existing 768px/`md` breakpoint) so the per-arrow score-entry buttons fit on screen without horizontal scrolling for the common WA 18m/25m preset (3 arrows per passe). Hide the Klasse column entirely below `md` (header + cells, display-only — the underlying `ScoreRow.className` data and prop stay untouched) and reduce header/data cell padding below `md`, while leaving `md:` and above (tablet/desktop) visually identical to today. The 44x44px minimum touch target on arrow buttons is a hard floor and must not change at any breakpoint.

Purpose: The trainer runs live score entry on a single phone at the range (CLAUDE.md: "Score entry ... must work correctly and offline, on one device"). Today ScoreTable relies on `overflow-x-auto` horizontal scrolling on phone widths, which is worse UX for rapid per-arrow tapping than fitting the essential columns on screen.
Output: Updated ScoreTable.svelte with responsive Klasse-column hiding and compact phone padding; a component-level class-presence assertion in ScoreEntry.test.ts; two new Playwright e2e assertions in scoring.spec.ts proving real-browser visibility behavior at 375px and 1024px.
</objective>
