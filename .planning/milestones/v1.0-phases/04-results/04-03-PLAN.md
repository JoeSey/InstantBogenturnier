<objective>
Extract the `isFinalized` boolean (currently inline in `ScoreEntry.svelte`) into a single shared pure function, and apply the RES-06 destructive-edit guard — disabled controls with an inline message (D-11: not an intercept-at-click-time dialog) — to delete-shooter (Registration), the rounds/passes config + shooting-line count (Setup/SetupRounds), and delete-class (ClassForm), triggered only once the tournament is finalized (D-12).

Purpose: Prevents the trainer from silently corrupting finalized tournament data by deleting shooters/classes or reconfiguring rounds after results have been locked, while explicitly leaving all *addition* workflows and the pre-finalization editing experience untouched (Pitfall 5).
Output: `computeIsFinalized` in `scoreCompletion.ts`; guarded controls + inline messages in four views; a new `Registration.test.ts` and extended `SetupRounds.test.ts`/`ClassForm.test.ts`/`scoreCompletion.test.ts`.
</objective>
