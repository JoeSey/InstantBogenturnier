| T-02-04 | Tampering | Shooting-line count & rounds/passes numeric inputs | mitigate | Enforce bounds before persisting: lines 1–10, passes-per-round 1–30, arrows-per-passe 1–20, rounds ≥1 (per 02-UI-SPEC.md "Validation approach") |
